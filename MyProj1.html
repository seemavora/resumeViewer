IBM Good Tech Scholar's First Place Winner




About the Project

In the summer of 2020, IBM hosted Good Tech Scholar's Program teaching students IBM cloud basics along with other fundamental cloud technologies. After a 2 week training period, students were asked to create a product utilizing the IBM cloud to solve the issue of educational inequality. Seema and her team, utilized the IBM Cloud APIs like Text-to-Speech as well as React.js and Node.js in order to create a full-scale project that helped students with disabilites learn in an online environment. Seema specifically worked on the homepage, creating a mock login and logout page, as well as assiting her teammate in creating a matching game. Her team ended up winning first place.
